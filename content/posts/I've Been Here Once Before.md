description: The clouds never lie

The sun are burning my skin. I move my legs as fast as I can. I'm tired, but still. No one need to know. I'm not a free man, but I wonder. I look to the sides, there's someone looking for me. I keep an eye over my shoulder until it rusts me. She warned me, oh but she warned me. I go wherever broken legs take me. Immovably unstoppable, always afraid but never stopping to search for the stars.
